Why Hockey?

Hockey is a fast-paced game. It is exciting all the way through unlike some other sports. It also has lots of physical contact if thats your thing. A major component of the game is checking. Checking is when you throw your body at someone but not like tackling in football. Checking is more throwing your shoulder or hip at someone to get them away from the puck.

Another big part of the game is fighting. While fighting will earn you a trip to the penalty box, that doesn't deter players from doing it almost every game. A fight, like a big hit, can completely change the momentum of the game. And it is exciting. Just like with big hits, there are many highlight reels of the best hockey fights of the past century.
